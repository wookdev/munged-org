WorldCon 2003 - Masquerade Site updated on  Sep 15, 2003
These are most of the Torcon 3 Masquerade entries. I was down in the greenroom, in an area called the "Fan Photo Area", where a few photographers can take pictures of the masquerade entries. There is also a official photographer, and an official videographer, but it is more fun to do it yourself. I was in the "available light" area, since I try to avoid camera-mounted flash as much as possible. "Available light", in this case, was 8, 500 watt, quartz-halogen floods. I sat in front and under them. The available light area is quite warm, and a very bright place to be. But despite the backdrop peeling away from the wall due to the tape heating up from the light, and a light hot-spot, I managed to get some nice photos of the masquerade.
