WorldCon 2003 - Assorted Photos Site updated on  Sep 11, 2003
Some photos taken during the "Fan Photo" shoot of the masquerade. These are not costumes. The belly dancers (cat women) came on to entertain us photographers while we were waiting for masquerade entrants to get ready and come be photographed. I'm not sure if they ever went on stage upstairs or performed anywhere else. I hope they did, so that others got to see them. The "Mounties" were reportedly escorts for the MC, and were also involved with helping the professional photographer in some way. Supposedly.
