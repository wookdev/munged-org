WorldCon 2003 - Fashion Show Site updated on  Sep 11, 2003
The TorCon3 Fashion Show. These were costumes done at previous WorldCons, CostumeCons, and regional masquerades. All are Canadian. They went on stage after the masquerade, and they trouped through the fan photo area as well. I didn't get all the Fashion entries, mostly due to a space problem on the camera. I did make sure I got the most impressive entries. I have zero details on who these people are, unfortunately. Sorry.
